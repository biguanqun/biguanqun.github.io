## SERI: Progressive Workflow for Empathetic Response Generation

SERI proposes a novel **SkEtching-Reasoning-Integrating** workflow for generating empathetic responses that consider both cognitive and affective aspects.

### Methodology

1. **Sketching**: Capture the initial response skeleton
2. **Reasoning**: Reason about multi-source information including emotions and context
3. **Integrating**: Dynamically integrate appropriate information for the final response
